01. About me

My path to software has been anything but ordinary. I studied English Translation in Iran, then spent years as an English teacher, a journalist and head video-game critic at SargarME, a guide writer at TheGamer, and a freelance graphic designer.

Today I'm in Naples pursuing a Master's in Language & Literature for Plurilingualism at Federico II, learning to build iOS apps at the Apple Developer Academy, and guiding travellers along the Amalfi Coast on the side. I bring a storyteller's eye and a designer's hands to everything I make.
