About

I draw my own art by hand in Procreate and build it into apps with Swift & SwiftUI.

I'm an iOS developer and designer based in Naples, trained at the Apple Developer Academy at the University of Naples Federico II. The years of writing and teaching before that never really left me, and they're a big part of why I think of myself as a designer who codes, with a writer's instinct.

Before Swift I taught English for five years, and I was always writing on the side too: study guides, criticism, editing. That's why I care as much about the words in an app as the buttons, and why something never feels done to me until it also reads right.

Draw. I draw everything by hand in Procreate: Persian gardens, studio characters, app icons, whole identities.

Build. Native iOS, iPadOS and watchOS in Swift and SwiftUI, built around the Human Interface Guidelines and proper accessibility.

Write. A teacher's and writer's ear behind every label, every line of copy, and the little story each app tells.
